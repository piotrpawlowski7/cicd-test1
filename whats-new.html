🔥 What's New on My Site! 🔥

Here's a log of all the updates I've made to this site! The newest stuff is at the top. I try to update at least once a week (when my parents let me use the phone line lol).

- ★ - ★ - ★ - ★ - ★ - ★ -

📅 1999

★ March 7, 1999 - MEGA UPDATE! ★

NEW VERSION 2.0 IS HERE!!! This is the biggest update EVER! I completely redesigned the whole site from scratch! New features include:

  • Brand new starfield background (no more boring white!)
  • Rainbow animated header - it changes colors!!
  • Double scrolling ticker bars (way cooler than <marquee>)
  • LED-style visitor counter with green glow
  • New sidebar with awards, counter, and links
  • Separate pages for About Me, Favorites, Links, and Guestbook!
  • Under construction banner with hazard stripes
  • Rainbow divider bar (SO COOL)

This took me like 3 weeks of working after school. I skipped a lot of homework for this. Worth it!

February 20, 1999

Started planning the v2.0 redesign! Drew sketches in my notebook during math class. Mrs. Johnson was NOT happy. Added some new entries to the guestbook page. Also updated my AIM screen name on the about page.

January 15, 1999

Happy New Year! We survived Y2K... wait, that's next year. Never mind. Added Fight Club and The Matrix to my movies list. Both movies BLEW MY MIND. If you haven't seen them, GO NOW. Also fixed a broken link on the links page (thanks to DragonSlayer for letting me know!).

January 1, 1999

HAPPY NEW YEAR 1999!! One more year until Y2K!! I stocked up on batteries and canned food just in case. Updated the copyright notice at the bottom. My resolution for 1999: learn JavaScript!

📅 1998

December 25, 1998

MERRY CHRISTMAS!! Got a new N64 game - The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time! I've been playing it non-stop for 3 days. It might be the greatest game ever made. Updated my games list accordingly. Also got 64MB RAM upgrade for my Gateway! Pages load SO much faster now.

November 20, 1998

Added Napster to my links page. You can download ANY song for FREE! I don't think it's illegal?? My friend Jake showed me and I already downloaded like 50 songs. Winamp is the best player btw. Also added Neopets to the links - I'm addicted to feeding my virtual pets.

October 31, 1998

Happy Halloween! 🎃 Added some spooky graphics to the header (took them down on Nov 1). Got a nice guestbook entry from someone dressed as Gandalf! Also joined the Awesome 90s Web Ring. We're already up to 47 sites!

September 12, 1998

School started again. Less time for website updates. Added Pokemon Red/Blue to my games list - I got Pokemon Blue for my birthday last month and it's INCREDIBLE. Chose Squirtle but now I kinda wish I chose Charmander. Updated my about page with new AIM screen name.

August 1, 1998

Summer update! Added the visitor counter (using a free CGI script from WebCounter). Also added a "best viewed with" notice. Netscape Navigator 4.0 is the BEST browser and I will fight anyone who says otherwise. Sorry IE users!

June 15, 1998

School's out for summer!! More time for website building! Added the guestbook feature. Please sign it! I'm so excited to see what people think. My mom was the first person to sign it which is kind of embarrassing but also sweet.

📅 1997

December 20, 1997

Added my favorites page with top 10 lists! Titanic just came out and it's AMAZING. Leo DiCaprio is SO dreamy. Also added the "cool links" section. Every good site needs a links page!

October 15, 1997

Big update! Added an about me section with my A/S/L, ICQ number, and quiz results. I'm Chandler Bing from Friends! Could that BE any more accurate?? Also learned how to use <TABLE> tags for layout. GAME CHANGER.

August 5, 1997

Added animated GIFs everywhere! Under construction signs, spinning email icons, and a dancing baby. Also figured out how to use <FONT> tags for colored text. The internet is SO COOL.

June 12, 1997

MY SITE IS LIVE!! 🎉 Just published my very first homepage on GeoCities! It's in the SunsetStrip neighborhood. Right now it just has a welcome message and some links but I have BIG plans. My BFF Sarah was the first visitor!! Taught myself HTML from "HTML For Dummies" - took me about 2 weeks. This is just the beginning!!
