🔥 My Totally Rad Links Page! 🔥

Here's my collection of the BEST sites on the entire World Wide Web! I've organized them by category for your surfing pleasure. If you want to do a link exchange, email me!

🔍 Search Engines

AltaVista

THE best search engine on the web. I use it every single day. Try the Babel Fish translator!

Yahoo!

Great web directory! Their categories make it easy to find stuff. Also check out Yahoo! Chat.

Ask Jeeves

You can ask questions in plain English! It's like talking to a real butler. The future is NOW.

Lycos

"Go get it!" Their search dog mascot is so cute. Good for finding images too.

Excite

Great start page with news, weather, and horoscopes. My homepage in Netscape!

HotBot

Powered by Inktomi. Really good at finding specific pages. The power search is awesome.

🏠 Free Homepage Hosting

GeoCities

Where I got my start! Pick a neighborhood (mine is SunsetStrip). 15MB free space!

Angelfire

Another great free host. Easy page builder if you don't know HTML yet (but you should learn!)

Tripod

Free hosting with a cool page builder. Their pop-up ads are kind of annoying though.

FortuneCity

Like GeoCities but with different neighborhoods. 25MB free! That's HUGE.

🎬 Entertainment & Fun

Space Jam Official Site

THE greatest website ever created. Michael Jordan + Looney Tunes. Click the ball to enter!

IMDB

Internet Movie Database - look up any movie or actor. Did you know it started on Usenet?

Neopets

Adopt virtual pets, play games, earn Neopoints! My Lupe is starving though, brb...

Hamster Dance

Dee da dee da dee da doh doh! The most addicting page on the internet. You've been warned.

Joe Cartoon

Hilarious Flash animations! The frog in the blender one is legendary. Needs Shockwave Plugin.

EbaumsWorld

Funny pictures, flash games, and videos. Warning: some stuff is NOT for kids!

💬 Chat & Communication

ICQ

"Uh oh!" Download the best instant messenger. My ICQ# is 7834291 - add me!

AOL Instant Messenger

AIM is where ALL my school friends hang out. Away messages are an art form.

mIRC

The best IRC client for Windows. Join #coolkids on EFNet! We're friendly, promise.

Hotmail

Free email! 2MB of storage - more than enough for all my emails. Thanks Microsoft!

🎵 Music & MP3s

Winamp

It really whips the llama's ass! Download cool skins. My playlist has 847 songs!

Napster

Share music with millions of people! Totally legal I think! Download anything in just 45 minutes!

MIDI Archive

Thousands of MIDI files! Great for adding background music to your homepage.

RealPlayer

Stream audio and video! The buffering is worth it, I promise. Mostly.

💻 Web Development

Lissa Explains it All

THE best HTML tutorial on the internet. Lissa taught me everything I know! 12 year old genius.

JavaScript Source

Copy-paste JavaScript for your page! Mouse trails, snow effects, popup alerts - SO COOL.

GIF World

Thousands of animated GIFs! Under construction signs, email icons, spinning globes, and more!

Web Counter

Add a free hit counter to your site! Choose from tons of digit styles. Gotta track those visitors!
