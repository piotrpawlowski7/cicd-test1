🔥 My Top 10 Lists! 🔥

Welcome to my favorites page! Here are my definitive top 10 lists for everything that matters. If you disagree with any of these, fight me in the guestbook! Just kidding... or am I? ;)

🎵 Top 10 Albums of ALL TIME

Currently playing in my Discman on repeat:

  1. Nevermind - Nirvana (1991) - Smells Like Teen Spirit changed my LIFE
  2. OK Computer - Radiohead (1997) - "Fitter, happier, more productive" is basically my AIM away message
  3. Ten - Pearl Jam (1991) - Eddie Vedder's voice is everything
  4. The Bends - Radiohead (1995) - Fake Plastic Trees makes me cry every time
  5. Mellon Collie and the Infinite Sadness - Smashing Pumpkins (1995) - THE double album to end all double albums
  6. Tragic Kingdom - No Doubt (1995) - Gwen Stefani is a QUEEN
  7. Jagged Little Pill - Alanis Morissette (1995) - When she said "You Oughta Know" I FELT that
  8. Dookie - Green Day (1994) - Basket Case is basically my theme song
  9. (What's the Story) Morning Glory? - Oasis (1995) - Wonderwall on repeat forever
  10. Spice - Spice Girls (1996) - Don't judge me, Wannabe is a BOP

🎬 Top 10 Movies

Movies I've watched approximately 9000 times on VHS:

  1. The Matrix (1999) - "There is no spoon." My brain is STILL melting
  2. Jurassic Park (1993) - Those dinosaurs looked SO REAL. Future of movies right here
  3. Pulp Fiction (1994) - Quentin Tarantino is a genius. Fight me.
  4. Titanic (1997) - "I'm the king of the world!" Cried for 3 hours straight
  5. The Lion King (1994) - Mufasa's death still haunts me. I'm not over it.
  6. Clueless (1995) - "As IF!" Cher is my fashion icon
  7. Fight Club (1999) - The first rule is I can't talk about it
  8. Space Jam (1996) - Michael Jordan AND Bugs Bunny? PERFECTION.
  9. The Sixth Sense (1999) - I DID NOT see that ending coming. NO SPOILERS!!
  10. Toy Story (1995) - My jaw was on the FLOOR. CGI is the future!

📺 Top 10 TV Shows

What I watch when I'm not surfing the web:

  1. Friends (1994-) - Could this BE any better? Answer: NO. Team Ross & Rachel forever
  2. The X-Files (1993-) - THE TRUTH IS OUT THERE. Mulder and Scully 4ever
  3. Seinfeld (1989-1998) - RIP to the greatest show about nothing. "No soup for you!"
  4. Buffy the Vampire Slayer (1997-) - Buffy is the ultimate girl power hero
  5. The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air (1990-1996) - Now this is a story all about how...
  6. Saved by the Bell (1989-1993) - Zack Morris is goals. That brick phone tho
  7. Boy Meets World (1993-) - Cory and Topanga give me hope for love
  8. South Park (1997-) - "Oh my God, they killed Kenny!" My parents would FREAK if they knew I watched this
  9. Dawson's Creek (1998-) - Joey and Dawson or Joey and Pacey?? I can't decide!!
  10. Pokemon (1998-) - "I wanna be the very best!" Theme song stuck in my head 24/7

🎮 Top 10 Video Games

Games that have consumed my entire life:

  1. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time (N64, 1998) - The GREATEST game ever made. Don't @ me.
  2. GoldenEye 007 (N64, 1997) - NO ODDJOB. That's the rule. Anyone who picks Oddjob is a cheater.
  3. Pokemon Red/Blue (Game Boy, 1998) - Gotta catch 'em all! I chose Charmander btw
  4. Super Mario 64 (N64, 1996) - "It's-a me, Mario!" in 3D!! My mind was blown
  5. Final Fantasy VII (PS1, 1997) - What happened to Aeris... I cried. I CRIED SO HARD.
  6. StarCraft (PC, 1998) - "You must construct additional pylons" haunts my dreams
  7. Super Smash Bros (N64, 1999) - Kirby main forever. Down+B all day baby
  8. Crash Bandicoot (PS1, 1996) - WOAH! That spinning attack is so satisfying
  9. Doom (PC, 1993) - My parents don't know I play this lol
  10. Oregon Trail (PC, 1990) - You have died of dysentery. EVERY. SINGLE. TIME.
